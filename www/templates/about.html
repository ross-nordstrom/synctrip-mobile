Learn

This project represents an interesting set of challenges for me. Additionally, it's a fun way to learn about a bunch of new technologies like AngularJS, Ionic, Firebase, and Cordova.

Plan

I do a decent amount of travelling. Combine that with my need to over-analyze and you shouldn't be surprised that I could get a lot of value from an app to figure out logistics for multi-destination trips.

Share

Beyond being tough to plan perfectly, complex trips can be difficult to share with family and friends. An aim of this project is to make it easy and seamless to communicate travel plans with automatic updates as changes are made.

Notify

Something I'd like to develop into this project is the ability for users to "subscribe" to a friend's itinerary. Subscribers will receive updates relevant to the current state of travel. One example would be text updates on your ETA:

"Trip Started 12:12pm - John's ETA is 6:22pm (in 6h)."
"Trip Update 3:12pm - Making good time! John's ETA is 6:12pm (in 3h)."
"Trip Update 4:12pm - Traffic delay! John's ETA is now 6:35pm (in 2h 23m)."
"Trip Update 6:05pm - John's ETA is still 6:35pm (in 30m)."

Track

Something I have a personal interest in is tracking my gas fillups. I find you can derive some interesting trends from looking at your historical gas such as changes in gas price, trends in your fuel economy, trends in your travelling. An extra feature I'd like to provide with this project is a database of gas prices, and your personal historical stops. By knowing where you've stopped in the past, the app can help you plan stops at your preferred gas stations or rest stops.
